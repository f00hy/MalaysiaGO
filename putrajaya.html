About Putrajaya

Putrajaya, Malaysia's federal administrative center, is a modern city renowned for its stunning architecture, expansive green spaces, and serene lakes. Known as the "Intelligent Garden City," Putrajaya is the epitome of urban planning, blending governmental functions with a high quality of life.

"Putrajaya is a symbol of Malaysia's vision for the future, where progress meets nature."


Urban Planning Specialist Jane Smith

From the iconic Putra Mosque to the expansive Putrajaya Botanical Gardens, this city offers visitors a unique experience of modern Malaysia, where tradition meets innovation. Its well-planned landscapes and futuristic buildings make it a must-visit destination for architecture enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.
