About Sabah

Sabah is a Malaysian state located on the northern part of Borneo. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Sabah is home to lush rainforests, vibrant coral reefs, and Mount Kinabalu, the highest peak in Southeast Asia. The state offers a unique blend of adventure, wildlife, and cultural experiences, making it a top destination for nature lovers and thrill-seekers.

"Sabah is where the wild meets the serene, offering an unforgettable journey into nature's heart."


Tourism Expert Jane Smith

From the majestic Mount Kinabalu that towers over the landscape to the crystal-clear waters of Sipadan Island, Sabah offers an array of outdoor activities and breathtaking views. Whether you're a diver exploring its rich marine life or an adventurer hiking through its rainforests, Sabah is a destination that promises both excitement and tranquility.

Featured Tourism Places

Mount Kinabalu

Located on the west coast of Sabah, Mount Kinabalu is the highest mountain peak in Malaysia, standing at a whopping height of 4101 meters.The fantastic views and the thrill urges visitors to go trekking on this mountain.

Sepilok Orangutan Rehabilitation Centre

Sepilok Orangutan Rehabilitation Centre (SORC) started in 1964 as rehabilitation for baby orangutans. It is under the administration of Sabah Wildlife Department with aims of returning orphaned, injured or displaced orangutans back into the wild.

Manukan Island

Experience the beauty of the Tunku Abdul Rahman Marine Park and enjoy sun, sea and sand surrounded by the natural surroundings. Guests can also take part in recreational activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving and parasailing, equipments are available for rent.

Kota Kinabalu City Mosque

The dome of the mosque is striking and resplendent in gold and blue colours with the structure resembling Al Masjid an Nabawi, the second holiest mosque in the world. It is surrounded buy an artificial lagoon that gave it the name of the floating mosque.

Borneo Kellybays

If you have not visited this aqua paradise in Sabah, your trip is incomplete. Situated amidst dense mangrove forest along the Southern China Sea, Borneo Kellybays is a perfect place to delve into the marine world. It is a must-visit spot for all the adrenaline junkies who just never get enough of adventure and fun.

Sapi Island

Sapi Island (Pulau Sapi) is on the itinerary of most island-hopping tours of Tunku Abdul Rahman Marine Park from Kota Kinabalu. Tours typically include round-trip transfers by boat and bus, and give you free time to swim, snorkel, and hike on between two and four of the islands.
