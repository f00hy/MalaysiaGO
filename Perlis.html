About Perlis

Perlis, the smallest state in Malaysia, is known for its serene landscapes, limestone hills, and rice fields. This lesser-known gem offers tranquility and unique cultural experiences.

"Perlis is a place where peace meets beauty."


Cultural Explorer Ahmad Yusof

Whether it's exploring limestone caves or wandering through rice paddies, Perlis promises an unforgettable experience for nature and culture lovers alike.

Venture into Gua Kelam, an ancient limestone cave with fascinating rock formations, a river flowing through it, and a rich history of tin mining.
